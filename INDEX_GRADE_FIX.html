‚ùì FAQ

Why do grades show wrong values?

A bug in the calculation formula divided percentages twice, making 93% show as 0.93%. We fixed the code, but your browser cached the old version.

Why must I clear cache?

Your browser cached the old buggy code before we fixed it. Clearing cache forces it to download the new fixed version.

Will this affect existing grades?

Old grades may need re-entry to use the correct formula. New grades entered after this fix will calculate correctly.

What if clearing cache doesn't work?

Try incognito mode to test, use a different browser, or contact your system administrator.
